The Business Opportunity

What Measurement Canada certification means

Gas stations are legally required to have every fuel pump inspected and certified by a Measurement Canada–authorized technician. Without a valid certificate, a pump cannot legally dispense fuel for sale. MMI will be authorized to issue those certificates.

What TSSA certification means

TSSA certifications authorize MMI to physically service, repair, and replace components on fuel dispensers and underground tank systems — the hands-on work that keeps pumps running between inspections.

Why having both is valuable

Right now, most Ontario gas stations hire two separate companies: one to inspect their meters (MC) and one to fix them (TSSA). MMI can be one call for both — simpler for the customer, more revenue per visit for us.

The market we're targeting

Ontario has roughly 2,200+ licensed fuel retail outlets. Most are independently owned. Many are underserved in Simcoe County and Muskoka. MMI is based in Wasaga Beach — well-positioned to serve this gap as a local, family-run operation.

The short version: Nicholas is building the credentials so MMI can legally walk into any gas station in Ontario and handle the full annual service cycle — inspect the pumps, certify them for trade, replace worn parts, and keep the site running. That's a recurring-revenue service business built on government-mandated certifications that take years to earn.
