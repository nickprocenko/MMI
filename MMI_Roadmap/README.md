# MMI Computers Inc. — Fuels Certification Roadmap

This repository tracks MMI's certification program under **Measurement Canada** (Weights and Measures Act) and **TSSA** (Technical Standards and Safety Authority). It is organized into two streams: administration and technician training.

## Sections

| Section | Purpose |
|---|---|
| [Admin](Admin/README.md) | MC agreements, Schedule A, client contracts, invoices |
| [Technicians](Technicians/README.md) | Course checklists, codebooks, study materials, field notes |

## Certification Pathway

```
NTTP Theoretical Exam
        ↓
  Practical (Field) Evaluation  ←  You are here
        ↓
  Added to Schedule A
        ↓
  Authorized to Inspect & Issue Certificates
        ↓
  TSSA Courses (expand service scope)
```

Technicians must complete both the theoretical and practical evaluations for each device type before being authorized to conduct examinations and issue certificates under their organization's registration.
