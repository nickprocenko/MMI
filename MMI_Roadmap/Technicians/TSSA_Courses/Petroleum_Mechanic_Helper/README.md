# Petroleum Mechanic Helper (PMH)

**Authority:** TSSA — Technical Standards and Safety Authority (Ontario)
**Provider:** Fuels Safety On-Line Inc. (fuelssafety.ca)
**Price:** $449.00 + $58.37 HST = **$507.37 total**
**Prerequisite:** None (but complete Liquid Fuels Handling Code first — recommended)
**Enables:** Replace nozzles, spouts, hoses, breakaways, filters, and belts on pumps and dispensers **without supervision**. This is the **mandatory prerequisite** for PM.1, PM.2, PM.3, and PM.4 — no one can receive a PM certificate without holding a PMH first.

**Status:** `NOT STARTED`

> **Do this second.** PMH is the gate to the entire Petroleum Mechanic pathway. The online course covers the theoretical portion; your practical experience and skills must be signed off by a currently licensed PM.1, PM.2, PM.3, or PM.4 before TSSA will issue the certificate.

---

## Enrollment

- [ ] Liquid Fuels Handling Code 2017 course completed (recommended first)
- [ ] Register at fuelssafety.ca → Petroleum Mechanic Helper (PMH) Training Course
- [ ] Purchase course ($507.37 total incl. HST)
- [ ] Receive course login / materials

## Certification Steps

- [ ] Complete all online PMH course chapters (short test at end of each chapter)
- [ ] Pass final TSSA-administered written exam (75% pass mark)
- [ ] Log required practical field hours under supervision of licensed PM.1/2/3/4
- [ ] Obtain sign-off from a licensed PM.1, PM.2, PM.3, or PM.4
- [ ] Apply for TSSA Certificate of Qualification
- [ ] Receive PMH certificate
