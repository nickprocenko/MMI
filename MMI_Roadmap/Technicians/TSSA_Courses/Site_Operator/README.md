# Site Operator (SO)

**Authority:** TSSA — Technical Standards and Safety Authority (Ontario)
**Provider:** Fuels Safety On-Line Inc. (fuelssafety.ca)
**Price:** $329.00 + HST per variant (~$372 total)
**Enables:** Legal operation of a fuel dispensing outlet. Three variants — choose the one matching your client type.

> **Note:** Site Operator certification does not cover minor maintenance (hose/nozzle replacement, etc.) — that requires PMH. These two certs work together.

**Status:** `NOT STARTED`

---

## Choose Your Variant

| Variant | Who It's For | Recommended? |
|---|---|---|
| **Retail Outlets** | Every station operator at a gas station, cardlock, keylock, or marina | **Yes — start here** |
| Private Fuel Outlets | Farmers, contractors, foresters, miners with private dispensing tanks | If serving agricultural clients |
| Municipal Outlets | Municipal employees at city fuel handling/dispensing facilities | Skip for now |

> **Recommended: Retail.** Most commercially relevant for gas station clients and is transferable knowledge to other variants.

---

## Enrollment

- [ ] No prerequisites required
- [ ] Choose variant: Retail / Private / Municipal
- [ ] Register at fuelssafety.ca → Site Operator Training Course (chosen variant)
- [ ] Purchase course (~$372 incl. HST)
- [ ] Receive course login / materials

## Certification Steps

- [ ] Complete all online course chapters (short test at end of each chapter)
- [ ] Pass TSSA-administered final exam
- [ ] Receive TSSA Site Operator Certificate of Qualification
- [ ] Obtain SO card / documentation
