# Petroleum Equipment Mechanic (PM.1 / PM.2 / PM.3)

**Authority:** TSSA — Technical Standards and Safety Authority (Ontario)
**Provider:** OPCA (Ontario Petroleum Contractors Association) or CPCA (Canadian Petroleum Contractors Association)
**Mandatory Prerequisite:** PMH certificate (no exceptions — TSSA will not issue any PM cert without it)

This folder covers the full Petroleum Mechanic (PM) certification pathway. There are three levels — pursue in order based on the work you want to do. **This is NOT a Red Seal trades apprenticeship** — it is TSSA's own certification program with self-arranged field hours.

---

## PM Levels — What Each Authorizes

| Level | Authorizes | Course Hours | Field Hours Required | Cost (non-member) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **PM.1** | Service & maintain dispensers, suction pumps, submersible pumps and related systems | 40 hrs | **1,000 hrs** | $700 course + $220 TSSA fee |
| **PM.2** | Install, remove, alter, test underground installations (tanks, piping) | 40 hrs | TBD | TBD |
| **PM.3** | Install, remove, alter, test aboveground installations | 40 hrs | TBD | TBD |

> Start with **PM.1** — most common day-to-day service work. PM.2 and PM.3 expand into tank and piping work for larger contracts.

**Status:** `NOT STARTED`

---

## How the PM.1 Pathway Works

**No formal apprenticeship.** You self-arrange your practical field hours with a licensed PM.

```
1. Hold valid PMH certificate
2. Enroll in PM.1 course (OPCA or CPCA — ~40 hrs home study)
3. Pass TSSA written exam (75% pass mark) via TSSA Client Portal
4. Log 1,000 hrs of field work under a licensed PM.1, PM.2, PM.3, or PM.4
5. Get sign-off on TSSA Practical Skills/Experience Sign-Off form
6. Submit application + signed form + $220 TSSA fee
7. Receive PM.1 Certificate of Qualification
```

> **Deadline:** You have **2 years from course completion** to submit your 1,000 hrs of practical experience. If you need more time, email certandexams@tssa.org before the deadline to request an extension.

## Enrollment (PM.1 — start here)

- [ ] PMH certificate obtained (mandatory)
- [ ] Identify a licensed PM to arrange field hours with (OPCA directory)
- [ ] Register for PM.1 course via OPCA ($700 non-member) or CPCA
- [ ] Receive course materials / access via TSSA Client Portal

## Certification Steps — PM.1

- [ ] Complete PM.1 home study course (40 hrs)
- [ ] Submit proof of training to training provider (for TSSA submission)
- [ ] Apply for TSSA written exam via Client Portal (certandexams@tssa.org)
- [ ] Pass TSSA written exam (75% pass mark)
- [ ] Log 1,000 hrs of field experience under licensed PM — within 2 years of course completion
- [ ] Obtain sign-off on TSSA Practical Skills/Experience Sign-Off form
- [ ] Submit application + sign-off + $220 TSSA fee
- [ ] Receive PM.1 Certificate of Qualification

- [ ] Register for PM.2 course
- [ ] Pass TSSA written exam
- [ ] Log required practical field hours under licensed PM
- [ ] Submit sign-off and fees to TSSA
- [ ] Receive PM.2 Certificate of Qualification
